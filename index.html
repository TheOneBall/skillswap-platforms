Frequently Asked Questions

How does skill exchange work?

Members offer skills they can teach and request skills they want to learn. We help match compatible learners and teachers in your area.

Is SkillSwap free to use?

Yes! Our basic platform is completely free. Members exchange skills directly without monetary transactions.

What skills can I learn or teach?

Almost anything! From cooking and music to programming and languages. If someone wants to learn it, you can teach it.
